# Example: AI Governance Monitoring

Representative monitoring targets:

- UN AI governance processes;
- scientific panels and global dialogues;
- EU AI Act implementation;
- US AI policy;
- national AI strategies;
- model release and safety developments;
- compute, evaluation, and multilingual equity debates.

Outputs should separate:

- fact;
- source;
- interpretation;
- relevance;
- confidence level;
- recommended follow-up.
