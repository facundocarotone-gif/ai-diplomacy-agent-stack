# Governance Checklist

Before publishing or acting externally:

- [ ] Is the action public, official, irreversible, or costly?
- [ ] Has the human operator approved it explicitly?
- [ ] Are sources reliable and cited where appropriate?
- [ ] Are private details removed?
- [ ] Are uncertainties marked?
- [ ] Could this be mistaken for an official position?
- [ ] Is there a rollback or correction path?
